First Voc | Brand Project Overview

First Voc is a specialized Egyptian hair care brand focused on providing innovative and effective solutions for hair health and scalp care. The brand offers a wide range of products including anti-hair loss treatments, nourishing serums, hair ampoules, daily care essentials, and customized hair care routines designed to meet individual customer needs.

Operating primarily through its online presence, First Voc aims to help customers achieve healthier, stronger, and more vibrant hair through scientifically developed products and personalized care solutions. The brand positions itself as a trusted choice for individuals seeking professional hair care products that deliver visible results.

With a growing digital community and a strong presence across social media platforms, First Voc continues to expand its reach among consumers interested in hair care, beauty, and personal wellness.

My Role & Contributions

As a Digital Marketing Specialist, I conducted comprehensive market research to evaluate the brand’s position within the hair care industry and identify opportunities for growth, differentiation, and stronger market penetration.

Key Tasks Completed
Market Research for First Voc

Conducted an in-depth market analysis covering:

Brand positioning within the Egyptian hair care market.
Target audience behavior and purchasing habits.
Customer needs and pain points related to hair loss and hair care.
Industry trends and emerging opportunities.
Product category demand and growth potential.
Digital presence evaluation and performance assessment.
Competitor Market Research

Performed a detailed competitor analysis to understand the competitive landscape and identify strategic opportunities for First Voc.

The analysis included:

Competitor brand positioning.
Product offerings and unique selling propositions (USPs).
Pricing strategies.
Social media performance and engagement.
Content approaches and communication styles.
Customer reviews and market perception.
Competitive strengths and weaknesses.
Opportunities for differentiation and growth.
Project Objective

The primary objective of the project was to gain a deeper understanding of the hair care market, evaluate First Voc’s competitive position, identify customer needs, and develop strategic insights that could support future marketing, branding, and business growth initiatives.

Outcome

The research provided valuable insights into market trends, customer behavior, and competitor strategies, helping establish a clearer roadmap for brand positioning, marketing opportunities, and future business development.
