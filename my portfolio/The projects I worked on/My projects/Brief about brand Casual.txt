Casual Shoes Egypt | Brand Project Overview

Casual Shoes Egypt is one of Egypt’s leading retail brands specializing in premium footwear and accessories. With a strong reputation for delivering high-quality shoes and bags, the brand combines contemporary fashion trends with exceptional craftsmanship to provide customers with stylish, comfortable, and durable products.

The brand has established a significant presence in the Egyptian market through an extensive network of 11 branches strategically located across Greater Cairo, including Heliopolis, Downtown, Hadayek El Zaitoun, El Zawya El Hamra, Nasr City, Shubra, Rehab City, Zamalek, Obour City, Mokattam, and El Zaitoun.

In addition to its retail presence, Casual has built a strong digital community with more than 306K followers on Facebook, 77.9K followers on Instagram, and 5.4K followers on TikTok, making it one of the recognized footwear and accessories brands in Egypt.

My Role & Contributions

As a Digital Marketing Specialist, I was responsible for analyzing the brand’s digital presence and developing strategic marketing plans to enhance brand awareness, customer engagement, and business growth.

Key Tasks Completed
Data Analysis

Conducted a comprehensive analysis of Casual's social media channels, audience behavior, engagement metrics, and overall digital performance to identify strengths, weaknesses, and growth opportunities.

Competitor Analysis

Researched and evaluated leading competitors in the Egyptian footwear and fashion retail market to benchmark performance, identify market trends, and uncover competitive advantages.

Action Plan Development

Created a strategic action plan outlining clear objectives, growth opportunities, content direction, campaign recommendations, and performance improvement initiatives.

Content Strategy

Developed a content strategy focused on increasing engagement, strengthening brand identity, showcasing products effectively, and supporting sales objectives across digital platforms.

Buyer Persona Development

Defined detailed buyer personas based on demographics, interests, purchasing behavior, and lifestyle characteristics to improve audience targeting and marketing effectiveness.

Media Buying Strategy

Designed a comprehensive media buying strategy including campaign objectives, audience segmentation, budget allocation, targeting structure, creative recommendations, and optimization plans to maximize campaign performance and ROI.

Project Objective

The project aimed to strengthen Casual's market position, improve brand awareness, increase customer engagement, and support long-term business growth through data-driven marketing strategies and strategic planning.

Outcome

The project delivered a clear and actionable marketing roadmap that provided valuable insights into customer behavior, competitive positioning, content opportunities, and advertising strategies, helping establish a stronger foundation for future growth and digital success.
