Diga Horand | Brand Project Overview

Diga Horand is a growing Egyptian women's fashion brand specializing in elegant evening wear, including dresses and blouses designed for young women seeking style, confidence, and affordability. The brand offers fashionable collections with prices ranging from 600 EGP to 900+ EGP, making trendy evening wear accessible to a wide audience.

One of Diga Horand’s key differentiators is its unique approach to styling by offering matching turbans with dresses, helping customers create complete and coordinated looks effortlessly. This added convenience strengthens the brand’s value proposition and enhances the overall shopping experience.

Operating under a B2C (Business-to-Consumer) model, Diga Horand sells directly to fashion-conscious women and actively utilizes social media platforms, influencer collaborations, and digital marketing campaigns to increase brand awareness and customer engagement.
