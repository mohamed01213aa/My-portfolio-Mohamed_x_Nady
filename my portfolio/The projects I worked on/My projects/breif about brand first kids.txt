First Kids | Brand Project Overview

First Kids is a well-established Egyptian children's fashion brand specializing in stylish, comfortable, and high-quality clothing for kids aged 2 to 14 years old. With more than 30 years of experience in the Egyptian market, the brand has built a strong reputation for delivering fashionable designs, premium fabrics, and affordable prices that meet the needs of modern families.

First Kids focuses on providing practical and trendy clothing collections that combine comfort, durability, and style, making it a trusted choice for mothers seeking quality apparel for their children.

The brand operates through multiple retail locations across Greater Cairo, serving families through both physical stores and digital platforms.

My Role & Contributions

As a Digital Marketing Specialist, I developed strategic marketing initiatives focused on increasing brand awareness, attracting potential customers, and supporting sales growth through content marketing, influencer collaborations, and paid advertising campaigns.

Key Tasks Completed
Content Plan Development

Created a comprehensive content plan designed to showcase product collections, seasonal offers, styling ideas, customer-focused content, and promotional campaigns aimed at increasing engagement and purchase intent.

Advertising Campaign Strategy

Developed targeted advertising campaigns across Facebook and Instagram to increase reach, generate engagement, drive store visits, and improve sales performance among mothers within the target audience.

Influencer Campaign Selection

Researched and selected suitable parenting influencers, family content creators, and lifestyle influencers whose audiences aligned with the brand's customer profile. The strategy focused on increasing trust, credibility, and brand awareness through authentic recommendations.

Campaign Planning & Recommendations

Developed campaign concepts and promotional strategies focused on seasonal collections, school seasons, holidays, special occasions, and sales periods to maximize customer engagement and revenue opportunities.

Project Objective

The project aimed to strengthen First Kids' digital presence, increase brand awareness among mothers, improve customer engagement, and support both online and offline sales growth through strategic content planning, influencer marketing, and performance-driven advertising campaigns.

Outcome

The project delivered a structured marketing framework that helped identify audience opportunities, strengthen brand communication, improve campaign effectiveness, and support long-term growth objectives within the children's fashion industry.
