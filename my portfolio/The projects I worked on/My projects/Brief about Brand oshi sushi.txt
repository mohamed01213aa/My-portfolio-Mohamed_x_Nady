Oshi Sushi Egypt | Brand Project Overview

Oshi Sushi Egypt is a prominent and specialized sushi restaurant based in Alexandria, recognized as one of the leading sushi brands in the city. Over the past two years, the restaurant has built a strong reputation by offering a diverse and innovative sushi experience tailored to customers seeking premium quality and exceptional taste.

The brand primarily targets Class B audiences, with a strong focus on young adults and women who appreciate modern dining experiences and high-quality Japanese cuisine. Oshi Sushi operates through two branches located in San Stefano and Stanley (Main Branch), serving a growing customer base across Alexandria.

Through its strong digital presence, Oshi Sushi has successfully built an engaged online community, positioning itself as a recognizable name within Alexandria's competitive restaurant industry.

My Role & Contributions

As a Digital Marketing Specialist, I conducted a comprehensive marketing analysis for Oshi Sushi to identify growth opportunities, improve brand positioning, and support the restaurant’s objectives of increasing sales, boosting order volume, and enhancing its social media presence.

Key Tasks Completed
Market Research

Conducted detailed market research to understand industry trends, customer preferences, audience behavior, and opportunities within the local restaurant and sushi market.

Page Appearance Analysis

Evaluated the visual identity and overall appearance of the restaurant's social media pages, providing recommendations to create a more consistent and professional brand image through a unified content grid and design structure.

Posting Frequency Analysis

Analyzed posting frequency across platforms and developed recommendations to improve content consistency, audience engagement, and brand visibility.

Platform Activity Assessment

Reviewed the performance and activity levels of each social media platform to identify the most effective channels for audience engagement and business growth.

Customer Interaction & Comment Analysis

Monitored audience feedback, comments, and customer interactions to identify common concerns, customer preferences, and opportunities to improve engagement and customer satisfaction.

Competitor Analysis

Conducted an in-depth analysis of key competitors in the sushi and restaurant industry to benchmark performance, evaluate content strategies, and identify competitive advantages for Oshi Sushi.

Top-Performing Content Analysis

Identified and evaluated the highest-performing posts to understand which content formats, themes, and creative approaches generated the strongest audience engagement.

7P's Marketing Mix Analysis

Developed a complete 7P's Marketing Mix analysis for Oshi Sushi, covering Product, Price, Place, Promotion, People, Process, and Physical Evidence.

Competitor 7P's Analysis

Analyzed competitors using the 7P's framework to compare market positioning, customer experience, pricing strategies, and promotional approaches.

SWOT Analysis

Conducted a comprehensive SWOT Analysis to identify strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats affecting the restaurant’s current market position and future growth potential.

Moderation Strategy

Reviewed customer communication processes and moderation practices, providing recommendations to improve response quality, customer engagement, and brand reputation.

Content Type Strategy

Analyzed and categorized content types across platforms, identifying the most effective content pillars and formats to support engagement, brand awareness, and sales objectives.

Project Objective

The primary objective of the project was to increase sales and order volume while enhancing the restaurant’s digital presence through strategic analysis, improved content planning, stronger customer engagement, and a more professional social media appearance.

Outcome

The project provided Oshi Sushi with valuable market insights, competitive benchmarks, audience behavior analysis, and actionable recommendations designed to improve brand positioning, strengthen customer relationships, optimize content performance, and support sustainable business growth.
