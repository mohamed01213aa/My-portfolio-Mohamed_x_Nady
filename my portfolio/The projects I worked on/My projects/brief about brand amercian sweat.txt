American Sweet | Brand Project Overview

American Sweet is a well-established Egyptian bakery and patisserie brand with a rich heritage dating back to 1986. Over the years, the brand has earned a strong reputation for delivering high-quality Eastern and Western sweets, combining authentic traditional flavors with modern creativity to satisfy a wide range of customer preferences.

The bakery serves families, young adults, and dessert lovers through multiple branches located in Nasr City, El Zeitoun, and Saray El Qobba, offering a diverse selection of freshly prepared desserts, pastries, and catering services for special occasions and celebrations.

With decades of experience in the Egyptian market, American Sweet continues to strengthen its position as a trusted destination for premium sweets and bakery products.
