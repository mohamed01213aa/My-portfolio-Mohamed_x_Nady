Ameely Travel | Brand Project Overview

Ameely Travel is a Category A travel and tourism company established in 2006, specializing in providing comprehensive travel services for both domestic and international destinations. Over the years, the company has built a strong reputation in the Egyptian tourism industry, serving more than 10,000 clients through a wide range of travel solutions tailored to different customer needs.

The company offers various tourism services including leisure tourism, business travel, religious tourism (Hajj & Umrah), domestic tours, international travel packages, and customized travel experiences. Ameely Travel is recognized for its commitment to customer satisfaction, educational travel content, and innovative marketing initiatives that help travelers make informed decisions.

With nearly two decades of experience, the company continues to position itself as a trusted travel partner for individuals, families, and corporate clients across Egypt.

My Role & Contributions

As a Digital Marketing Specialist, I conducted market research and developed strategic content initiatives to strengthen the company's digital presence, improve audience engagement, and support brand awareness objectives.

Key Tasks Completed
Market Research

Conducted detailed market research to analyze:

The tourism industry landscape in Egypt.
Customer travel behavior and preferences.
Target audience segments and travel motivations.
Market opportunities and growth potential.
Competitive positioning and industry trends.
Content Plan Development

Created a structured content plan designed to:

Increase brand awareness.
Educate audiences about travel services.
Promote tourism packages and seasonal offers.
Build trust and credibility among potential travelers.
Improve engagement across social media platforms.
Important Tourism Calendar Events Selection

Identified and selected two key annual events and seasonal opportunities relevant to the travel industry to maximize campaign effectiveness, increase audience engagement, and support promotional activities.

UGC Video Reel Creation

Developed User-Generated Content (UGC) video reel concepts focused on:

Showcasing customer travel experiences.

Increasing engagement through relatable content formats.
Supporting awareness and conversion objectives.
Project Objective

The project aimed to strengthen Ameely Travel’s digital presence, improve audience engagement, increase awareness of its tourism services, and create content that effectively communicates the company’s expertise and travel solutions to diverse customer segments.

Outcome

The project delivered valuable market insights, audience-focused content planning, and creative content recommendations that supported the company's marketing objectives and enhanced its ability to connect with potential travelers through digital platforms.
